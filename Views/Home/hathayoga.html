What is Hatha yoga

Hatha Yoga is a foundational style of yoga that focuses on physical postures (asanas) and breath control (pranayama) to create a balanced and harmonious union within oneself. It lays the groundwork for various yoga disciplines, making it an ideal starting point for beginners and a valuable practice for seasoned yogis.
